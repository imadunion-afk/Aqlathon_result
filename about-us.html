About Us

IMAD, established on December 15, 2012, is dedicated to the holistic development of students. Striving for complete academic and co-curricular progression, we create pathways for learning, skill-building, and personal growth. Serving the students of SFADC, IMAD provides modern facilities and programs designed to meet the demands of today’s educational landscape.

Our focus extends beyond academics to nurturing talents, encouraging creativity, and fostering leadership, ensuring students are well-prepared for life beyond the classroom. At IMAD, empowering potential and fostering achievement is our core commitment.

Core Values

Our Mission

To provide students with opportunities for leadership, learning, and personal growth rooted in Islamic principles. We promote academic excellence, community engagement, and teamwork while nurturing talent and preserving our Islamic identity.
